## What RAR does

- **Repair** — board-level diagnostics on phones, laptops, and consumer electronics. Bilingual intake (English / Spanish) for Houston clients.
- **Web** — small-business websites and landing pages, built to load fast and stay maintainable.
- **Advertising** — brand and ad work for the same kinds of clients that come in for repair.

## Why one studio, three services

Most local shops split repair, web, and advertising across three vendors who don't talk. The client ends up translating between them. RAR closes that loop: same hands on the hardware, the site, and the campaign.
