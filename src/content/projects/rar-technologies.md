## What RAR Technologies does

- **Website design and development** — static and interactive sites, published on GitHub Pages or the client's chosen host.
- **Technology-product development** — small tools, embedded projects, and hardware-software integration.
- **Electronics repair** — board-level diagnostics and repair for phones, laptops, and consumer electronics.
- **Hardware diagnostics and software troubleshooting** — narrow down the failure, restore the device, document the fix.
- **Device restoration and embedded electronics** — bringing hardware back to spec, and building new hardware from scratch.
- **Technical documentation** — plain-English (and plain-Spanish) documentation of what was done and why.

## How the work runs

Client intake happens in English or Spanish. Every job — a website, a repair, or a product build — is scoped, delivered, and documented by the same person. GitHub Pages publishing and site maintenance are part of the standard web engagement so clients don't have to manage hosting.
