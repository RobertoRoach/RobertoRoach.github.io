## Coaching

Coached VEX IQ teams and mentored students on VEX V5 through iCode Memorial. The coaching work covered:

- Robot construction and mechanical design
- Autonomous programming
- Distance, color / optical, inertial, touch, and AI vision sensor integration
- Motor control and movement strategies
- Debugging, testing, and competition strategy

## Sub-judging and volunteering

Sub-judged and volunteered at VEX IQ competitions during the Mix & Match 2025–2026 season. Reading a rubric from the judges' side teaches you what a clear, defensible design explanation actually sounds like — which shapes how I coach my own students to speak to judges.
