## Coaching, not directing

The Odyssey of the Mind rules are strict about outside help: the ideas have to come from the students. My role was to run the practice, ask the questions, and keep the team honest about schedule and scope. When the team wins, the team wins — the coach's job is to have made the questions sharp enough that they got there.

## Why this matters for engineering

Judged competitions are a good stress test for the engineering itself. If you can't explain a design choice to a judge under time pressure, you also can't explain it to a client, an admissions officer, or a hiring manager. The practice sharpens my own work more than any class does.
