## Scope

Coursework — a high-school aerospace class. Not a formal rocketry competition.

## What the work covered

- **Rocket design** — including center-of-mass considerations and stability.
- **Construction** — full assembly of the airframe and integration with the motor stage.
- **Launch preparation and launch.**
- **Post-launch analysis** — reading recovered rockets and flight behavior against the pre-flight expectations.
