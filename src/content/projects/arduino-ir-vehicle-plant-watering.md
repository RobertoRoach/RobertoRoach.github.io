## The builds

- **Arduino IR-controlled miniature vehicle** — an infrared-driven vehicle covering motor control and IR communication.
- **Automated plant-watering system** — sensor-driven actuation for a real household use case.
- **Sensor-based electronics** — smaller exploratory builds around common Arduino sensors.
- **Bluetooth and audio experiments** — the foundational work that later fed into the [Arduino Embedded Audio System for Odyssey of the Mind](/work/arduino-embedded-audio/).
